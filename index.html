What is T-ONLINE?

T-online is a free, cloud-based tool for monitoring servers. Monitor your servers and back-end infrastructure every 5 minutes and get alerted if they go down.

Follow the steps below to install T-online on your server:
  • You must have a T-online product key to use our service. You can get a free key from our telegram bot. Start a chat with the bot and send a identifier for your server. We'll send you a key server as soon as possible

START CHAT WITH BOT

    • SSH into your server

    • That's it. Now you will get all updates on the notification channel.
